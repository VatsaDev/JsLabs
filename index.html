Document.write

Both Jsbin and Jsfiddle use document.write multiple times in the immediate front, slowing you down. JSlabs uses data-urls in the background, to speed up your usage.

Speed

Codepen has to go to a cloud, build files on servers, and then serve them. This with millions of pens at the same time. All of jslabs is local, serving your stuff to you only, and no one else, with data urls.
